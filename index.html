the cat, the myth, the legend

mochi is a stray cat discord bot who specializes in casino games but also offers wholesome services like daily fortune cookies, user stat horoscopes, and treat allowances. those degenerate enough to gamble with mochi can climb their way up his global ranks by collecting treats from their winnings and spending them sparingly in his shop.

you can command mochi to do your dirty (or clean) bidding. or if you're normal, you can pet him, play with him, and dote on him like you would an actual cat. you can attempt to get mochi's attention by saying his name or meowing. you can also get mochi to rate anything in the world by feeding it to him.

commands

  • access his command guide in discord with .mochi
  • feed with .feed (ex .feed salmon)
  • interact with .pet, .play, or .walk
  • make mochi do your bidding with .kiss, .hug, .cuddle, .slap, .punch, or .kill (ex .cuddle @user)
  • get your daily stats with .stats
  • claim a daily fortune cookie with .cookie or .fortune
  • claim daily treats with .treats
  • view total treats with .bank
  • give treats to someone with .slide (ex .slide @user 50)
  • play blackjack with .cards (bet more by adding a number, ex .cards 25)
  • play slots with .slots (bet more adding a number, ex .slots 25)
  • access mochi's shop with .shop
  • view mochi's global fattest cat hit list with .rank
